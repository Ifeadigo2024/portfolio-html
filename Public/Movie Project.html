My top five(5) best movies of all time

  • The Godfather" (1972) - Crime drama directed by Francis Ford Coppola.
  • The Shawshank Redemption" (1994) - Inspirational drama set in a prison.
  • The Dark Knight" (2008) - Gripping superhero film with Batman and the Joker.
  • Pulp Fiction" (1994) - Quentin Tarantino's iconic crime film with nonlinear storytelling.
  • Inception" (2010) - Mind-bending science fiction exploring shared dreaming.
